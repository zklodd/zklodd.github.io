Hobbies

Some hobbies and interests I have can vary by season. In the spring/summer, I love to golf and spend my time outside, I've been golfing for a long time and although I don't take it too seriously, it makes for a good way to spend my free time enjoying the beautiful weather. During the wintertime I will go skiing and snowboarding, as long as there is snow on the ground, I will be at the Alpine Valley Ski Resort doing one of the two things, I have been skiing for about 5 years and just got into snowboarding, but they are both exhilarating and a ton of fun!

On the days I don't get to spend outside or it being out of season, I enjoy going out with friends, most of the time we will go bowling, but every once and a while there will be a new thing to pick up. When I spend time on my own I like to play video games to pass the time!
